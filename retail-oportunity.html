Retail Opportunities

Intelligent investors are always looking for newer and more modern ways to invest and make best use of the market. Our firm has achieved this by providing innovative alternative investments and projects that are low risk with returns better then the bank and capital market.

As investors are becoming more familiar with alternative investments and their benefits, the demand has increased. With increasing demand our firm looked into the retail sector with a niche that caters 600 million dollars worth of demand per annum.

Our team is working very closely in the retail investment niche to solve a problem that had been present for decades without market players paying close attention. With our experience and in-depth knowledge our team is not only able to solve the problem but has also partnered with intelligent firms that can offer our clients guaranteed returns and protection of capital. One famous quote explains basically the viewpoint we have on the finding niches for our partners:

“How many millionaires do you know who have become wealthy by investing in savings accounts? I rest my case.” – Robert G. Allen

Following this ethos we always encourage our clients to learn how to invest in retail and explore the segments of the markets that are unconventional. Exploring such innovative segments allows our firm and our partners to always stay ahead of the market.

WATER3

WATER3

As one of our flagship investments Water3 is one of our most successful projects. Since its inception in 2012 Water3 has acquired international attention and are now going global.


Water3 Kiosk by Chatstone

Chatstone’s goal is to revolutionise the way we as a community consume bottled water. The bottled water market is a $60 billion industry world wide. With this size market brings with it a huge amount of waste in our landfills and water ways from the single use disposable plastic bottles. Your investment not only provides you with a low risk, stable return, it will also contribute to reducing the amount of pollution and waste that traditional bottled water creates.
